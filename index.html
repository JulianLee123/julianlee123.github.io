Hi, I'm Julian Lee

I'm a rising Junior at Yale University pursuing a 4-year combined B.S./M.S. in Computer Science with a certificate in Data Science. I'm passionate about machine learning, and I am constantly looking for opportunities to learn new techniques and technologies while tackling impactful problems. Through research and summer internships at American Express and the Naval Research Laboratory, I have engineered machine learning solutions in areas including robotics, recycling, and secure software development. This work has led to the publication of 3 first-author papers. I'm excited to continue solving challenging problems in this rapidly growing field.
